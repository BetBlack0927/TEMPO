Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Tempo Pulse

How does Tempo Pulse learn my patterns? expand_more
Tempo Pulse uses advanced machine learning to analyze your daily routines, meeting patterns, and travel times. It continuously improves its predictions based on your actual behavior and feedback, making it increasingly accurate over time.
What calendar systems are supported? expand_more
We support all major calendar systems including Google Calendar, Microsoft Outlook, and Apple Calendar. More integrations are being added regularly based on user feedback and requirements.
How does the energy level optimization work? expand_more
The app tracks your meeting patterns, focus times, and productivity peaks to suggest optimal scheduling of different types of activities. It considers factors like meeting intensity, break requirements, and your natural energy cycles throughout the day.
Is my data secure and private? expand_more
Yes, we take security and privacy seriously. All data is encrypted, and we follow strict privacy guidelines. You have full control over what data is collected and how it's used. We never share your personal information with third parties.
Can I customize the notifications? expand_more
Absolutely! You can fully customize notification types, timing, and frequency. Set preferences for different types of events, choose how far in advance you want to be notified, and specify quiet hours when you don't want to be disturbed.
